<div class="post-lang-content" data-post-lang="en" markdown="1">

I'm going to write more posts in 2026. More posts means lower quality for each one, but I think the overall value will increase. Writing is hard, and the only way to really practice is to write more.

Based on the [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE) concept (Publish on your Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere), I'll sync my posts to X for English content and WeChat 朋友圈 for Chinese content.

I'll provide both English and Chinese versions of my posts—English first, then Chinese.

</div>

<div class="post-lang-content" data-post-lang="zh-CN" markdown="1">

2026年我打算多写一些文章。数量多了，单篇质量可能会下降，但我认为整体价值会提升。写作本来就难，唯一的练习方法就是多写。

基于 [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE)（先发布在自己的网站，再同步到其他平台）的理念，我会把英文内容同步到 X，中文内容同步到微信朋友圈。

我的文章会提供中英双语版本——先英文，后中文。

</div>
